Research

I began working with Dr. Elisabeth Mills at the University of Kansas in my sophomore year. In my project I analyze ALMA data of the hot protostellar core Cloud C within the Galactic Center. The goals of our project are to get accurate masses for several sources within Cloud C and to assess the composition of the cloud. Through observing multiple CO isotopologues we are able to calculate more accurate masses than have been determined by the dust continuum as it suffers a fundamental uncertainty on the dust temperature. We seek to assess the composition of the cloud to characterize the initial conditions of massive star formation in this unique region of the Galaxy.

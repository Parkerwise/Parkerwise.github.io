Preventing Burnout

02-08-24

I have noticed that at the beginning of every semester I come in with a lot of enthusiasm and am super productive and then after a month or two I start to get burnt out. I think a large part of this has to do with has to do with the fact that I need to have time to decompress everyday, and my routine had me working 24/7. I have started a new routine that I hope will help prevent burnout. I have been doing work at school and getting on campus at ~6 am. This gives me 2-3 hours in the morning where I still have enough "spoons" for the day to get challenging and tedious work done without distraction. This allows me to keep a work life balance since I'm not working at home, and it allows me to actually relax after school since by the time classes are over most of my work is usually done. Let's hope this routine works!
